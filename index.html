Captain Marvel

When head of NASA security Carol Danvers was exposed to a powerful alien device, she transformed into the mighty cosmic powered hero, Captain Marvel!


Flight in space

After an alien device mutated her DNA, Danvers transformed from a merely brilliant pilot into one of the most powerful Super Heroes in the universe. Now soaring among the stars, Carol Danvers is known as Captain Marvel!
Captain Marvel has the power of flight, which she uses in tandem with her ability to breathe in the vacuum of space to journey at super speed throughout the cosmos.

Binary Powers

Carol’s Binary powers, though largely dormant, can bestow her with the might of a white hole, allowing her to generate and control any form of radiation or gravity

Black Widow

A deadly assassin is closing in on Natasha Romanoff. Now Natasha must reunite with an unlikely group of spies from her past in order to survive and stop a lethal force from being unleashed on the world.


Master Fighter

She is extensively trained to near perfection in various fighting styles, such as karate, judo, kung fu, and wrestling. Combining all manner of weapons with her background in gymnastics, acrobatics, and ballet, she always accomplishes her mission.

Strength and Agility

Exhibiting no superhuman abilities, the Black Widow boasts ultimate human levels of strength, agility, and stamina.

Widow's BITE

Her signature weapons include dual batons, a pair of Glock 26s and the Black Widow’s Bite—bracelets that discharge electricity.

Thanos

The Mad Titan Thanos quests across the universe in search of the Infinity Stones, intending to use their limitless power for shocking purposes.


Alliance with LOKI

Thanos directed the Other to make contact with Loki of Asgard to bequeath a special scepter to him that he might use to wreak havoc on Earth.

Retrie ving the ORB

Thanos directed Ronan’s mission to retrieve the powerful Orb and tasked Gamora and Nebula to aid him; unfortunately for the Titan, all three turned against him in the course of events that followed and he lost the Infinity Stone housed in the Orb.

Death on Vormir

On Vormir, Thanos, in tears, killed his daughter and was then gifted with the Soul Stone—confirming his love for Gamora, even as he ended her life.

Iron Man

Genius. Billionaire. Philanthropist. Tony Stark's confidence is only matched by his high-flying abilities as the hero called Iron Man.


SUIT UP

In a cave in Afghanistan, Tony Stark and Ho Yinsen developed the first Iron Man suit, which Stark used to escape his captors.

Saving NEW YORK

Fighting alongside the Avengers for the first time, Iron Man nearly sacrificed himself in order to save New York

Near DEATH

Stark was nearly killed when an attack by the supposed Mandarin destroyed his home, sending him plummeting into the sea.


Already on opposite sides of a fight over the Sokovia Accords, Iron Man unleashed his full fury on Captain America and Bucky when he learned the truth about his parents’ death.
